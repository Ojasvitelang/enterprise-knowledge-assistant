Company Training Policies

Welcome to our Education and Training Institute. This document outlines the key policies that govern our training programs.

Enrollment Policy

All students must complete the online registration form before attending any training session. Registration closes 48 hours before the scheduled start date. Late registrations may be accepted on a case-by-case basis with approval from the program coordinator.

Students must provide valid identification and proof of eligibility for the specific program they are enrolling in. Corporate-sponsored students must submit authorization letters from their employers.

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is mandatory for all training programs. Students must maintain a minimum of 80% attendance to be eligible for certification. Absences must be reported to the training coordinator within 24 hours.

Three consecutive unexcused absences may result in automatic withdrawal from the program. Make-up sessions are available for students with valid medical or emergency reasons.

Assessment and Certification

All programs include both theoretical and practical assessments. Students must achieve a minimum score of 70% in all assessments to pass. Failed assessments can be retaken once within 30 days.

Certificates are issued within 14 business days of successful program completion. Digital certificates are provided immediately, while physical certificates require additional processing time.

Refund Policy

Full refunds are available if cancellation is made 7 days before the program start date. 50% refunds are available for cancellations made between 3-7 days before start. No refunds are available for cancellations made less than 3 days before the program begins.
